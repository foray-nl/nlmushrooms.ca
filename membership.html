Membership



Download Membership Form: Effective January 2024


Membership is usually paid with foray registration, but is available separately for those unable to participate, but still wishing to be part of the organization.

Membership is effective from opening day of closest main annual foray following payment to opening day of the subsequent annual foray. One fee covers spouse/partner and any underage children, all living at same address.


Membership fees can also be made via e-transfer to  foraynltreasurer@gmail.com  as indicated on the form.



Benefits of Membership


  • Advance notice of Foray (last few years we have not been able to accommodate all registrants).
  • Kept informed of forays and activities of FORAY NEWFOUNDLAND & LABRADOR.
  • Year's subscription to OMPHALINA, the electronic newsletter/journal of FNL.
  • A direct voice in how the foray is run and who runs it.
  • Add your voice to the number of organized naturalists interested in our nature.
