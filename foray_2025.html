Foray 2025

Pinchgut Lake

Western NL (Corner Brook area)

September 26–28, 2025

Gear up for a unique outdoor experience with Foray NL. Taking place September 26th–28th 2025 at Pinchgut Lake south of Corner Brook. The 2025 Newfoundland mushroom and lichen foray is an annual survey to develop a list and collection of what species live across the island.

This annual Foray NL event is a great way for you to connect with others in your community while learning about mushrooms and lichens. No experience is required, but a healthy curiosity for the natural world is encouraged!

Member registration is opening mid May with Public registration to follow in early June. The cost is $250 per person for the weekend, and children under 12 are free!

Prepare to come together with scientific researchers, avid citizen scientists, and curious minds passionate about the natural world. You will have the chance to participate in exciting foraging sessions, attend insightful lectures and interactive workshops.

Get ready to immerse yourself in a weekend of food, friends and fungi on Newfoundland's west coast.

Please note that waitlist spaces are limited.

Please submit your registration form, but hold payment until your registration is confirmed by the Foray NL Treasurer. All additional registration submissions will be added to the waitlist in case someone backs out.
