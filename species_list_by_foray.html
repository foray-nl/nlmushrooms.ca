Below are links to PDF files that contain lists of species collected at various Forays since Foray Newfoundland and Labrador started in 2003. In recent years, the lists were included in Foray Reports. These are been extracted from the reports and and included here as separate documents. More recent species lists also include lichens.


Species lists by Foray are useful when searching for mushrooms in areas previously covered by Foray NL. In recent years, the mushrooms are listed by trail. These reports give those interested an indication of what might be found in a more specific locale than the cumulative list which covers the whole province.
